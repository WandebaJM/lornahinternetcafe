About Lornah Internet cafe

Lornah Internet Cafe was established in 2008. It handles all data needs of the all research projects. The department has different platforms on which the data is managed depending on the projects budget. The staff is experienced in handling data from different clients. It has a good reputation and is known for its ability to manage data from cross sectional and cohort studies.



Lornah Internet Cafe offers a variety of solutions ranging from paper to paperless and remote application access and administration. Lornah Internet Cafe utilizes the most recent operating systems and provides extended support of all systems we build. The Data Center works towards ensuring that the technology being employed are tested and is one of the best solution for the project in which it is being used.



The data management systems we use include: MS ACCESS, MS Word, MS Excel, MS Power-point among others. The data is stored on safely secure Windows server where it is backup daily.
